About

Avangard Innovative is a sustainability company that has been providing profitable waste solutions to mid-level to fortune 500 companies. They gave me the opportunity to re-designing their website in order to create a more modern and innovative impression. The goal was to tell the story of Avangard that delivered to message in a succinct and clear manner.
