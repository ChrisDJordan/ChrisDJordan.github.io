Web Design isn’t just my job, it’s my Adventure.

I'm Chris Jordan, a Web Designer located in the beautiful state of Texas. I enjoy using logic and design to create engaging experiences that bring confidence to my clients and happiness to their users.

I recently graduated the User Interface Design course at The Iron Yard in Austin, Texas. Since then I've continued my dream as a Web Designer soaking up as much knowledge as I can and contributing back into the world.

I like to look at every project as an adventure, each involving amazing people with different backgrounds and stories. I take great passion in my field and love to work on projects big or small.
