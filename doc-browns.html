Doc Browns

About

This project was for doc Browns in San Antonio. It was the second buiness the family was opening with a different atmosphere than the first.they wanted a clean up to date website and to be able to showcase there food as well as have a section for the chef to put press items and blog post. Because of the short time frame until the restaurants grand opening i had to make fast decisions and create concepts quickly. I worked with a front-ender to breakup some of the work load while work on other projects and we used sqaurespaces cms for ease of use and the clients prior knowledge.

Technologies Used

  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • Sqaurespace

Process

With a logo storyboard and competors in the area I started with very little direction. Aiming to meet the clients time frame and must haves, my concepts were clean, light and elegant. With the time crunch at hand I Focusing on the main typographical points and color pallets. I used the menu and the navigation in my styletile to show some of the possible layouts

With a logo, storyboard, and competors in the area I started with very little direction. I knew they wanted a clean easy to use design.
