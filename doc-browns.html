About

This project was for Doc Browns in San Antonio. It was the second business the family was opening with a different atmosphere than the first. They wanted a clean, up to date website where they would be able to showcase their food as well as have a section for the chef to put press items and blog post. Because of the short time frame until the restaurants grand opening I had to make fast decisions and create concepts quickly. I worked with front-ender Zack Chaney to breakup some of the work load while working on other projects. We used Sqaurespace's CMS for ease of use and the clients prior knowledge.

Technologies Used

  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • Sqaurespace

Process

With a logo, storyboard and competitors in the area I started with very little direction. Aiming to meet the client's time frame and must haves, my concepts were clean, light and elegant. With the time crunch at hand I focused on the main Typographical points and color palettes. I used the menu, and the navigation in my style tile to show possible layouts.

The client felt that the third style tile was the right direction for the website, and we moved on to layout.
